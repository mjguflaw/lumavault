Lumavault Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2026

Lumavault is designed with privacy in mind. The app helps users review and clean up their photo libraries while keeping control in the user’s hands.

Photo Library Access

Lumavault requests access to your photo library so it can scan your photos and videos locally on your device.

Lumavault does not upload your photos or videos to our servers. Photo and video analysis is performed on-device.

Data Collection

Lumavault does not collect personal data from users through the app.

Lumavault does not upload your photos or videos, sell your data, share your data with advertisers, track you across apps or websites, or use your photos to train AI models.

Purchases

Lumavault may offer a one-time Pro unlock through Apple’s in-app purchase system. Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Support Requests

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, device information, screenshots, or a description of the issue.

We use support information only to respond to your request and improve the app.

Third-Party Services

Lumavault uses Apple frameworks and services, including PhotoKit, Vision, AVFoundation, and StoreKit, to provide app functionality on your device.

Your use of Apple services is subject to Apple’s privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

Lumavault is not intended to collect information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
