Frequently Asked Questions

Does Lumavault delete photos automatically?

No. Lumavault does not automatically delete photos. The app suggests items for review, but you decide what to keep, queue, or delete.

Where do deleted photos go?

When you confirm deletion, iOS moves photos to the Recently Deleted album in the Photos app. Apple controls the final deletion behavior through Photos.

Does Lumavault upload my photos?

No. Lumavault scans your photo library locally on your iPhone. Your photos are not uploaded to Lumavault servers.

Does Lumavault use my photos to train AI?

No. Lumavault does not use your photos to train AI models.

Why does Lumavault need photo access?

Lumavault needs photo access so it can scan your library locally and show cleanup sessions for your review.

Can I use Limited Photos access?

Yes. If you choose Limited Photos access, Lumavault can only review the photos you select.

Is Lumavault a subscription?

No. Lumavault is free to download and offers a one-time Pro unlock. No subscription is required.
